I am an applied scientist at Amazon Alexa AI. Before that, I received a Ph.D in Statistics at Univeristy of Pittsburgh, a Master in Statistics at University of California Berkeley, and a Bachelor in Management Science at KAIST. Prior to the graduate study, I worked as a data scientist at Korea Credit Bureau (KCB) for 5+ years.
My main research interest is to quantify the uncertainty in context of Natural Language Processing (NLP). I am also broadly interested in ML/DL applications on NLP problems.
